## My goal is to help users by listening to their needs and then provide them with the best solutions, so that they can have the best experience.

I am a UX Researcher & Engineer who is currently pursuing a Bachelors in Computer Science at the Georgia Institue of Technology. I strive to create awesome experiences by organizing information and making it universally accesible. To achieve this, I believe it is important to be humble and empathetic, to design with accessibility in mind, and to recognize that mistakes will happen and rather then let that possibility scare you, be able to learn from failure. I have a great deal of experience in team enviornments, and as a result, I am highly adaptable in both independent and collaborative settings. I consider my hunger for both continuous development and feedback to be some of my strong points, and some of my skills include UI/UX Design and Research, user testing, prototyping, and content creating.

In my free time, I enjoy filming and editing vlogs and travel videos for my personal YouTube channel, and I also enjoy spending time at public gardens and museums. I make it a point to visit one whenever I travel to a new city! My favorite experience so far was visiting the Chihuly Garden and Glass museum two years ago in Seattle, and I had the pleasure of seeing it again at the Biltmore in Asheville, North Caroline last summer. One of my favorite things to do is try out good places to eat good food, and recently, I've started trying my hand at baking.

You've heard about me, now let's talk about you. Send me a <a href="mailto:saeunx@gatech.edu">hello</a>. 👋
