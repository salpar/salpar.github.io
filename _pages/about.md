## My goal is to help users by listening to their needs and then provide them with the best solutions, so that they can have the best experience.

Hello! My name is Sally, and I am a UX Researcher & Engineer currently pursuing a Bachelor’s in Computer Science at the Georgia Institute of Technology. As a lover of all things design and development, I have the potential to make a huge impact with my strong communication and verbal skills, passion for challenging myself, and impressive attention to detail. I strive to create awesome user experiences by collecting user insights and applying it to new opportunities for user improvement, and to achieve this, I believe it is important to be humble and empathetic, to design with accessibility in mind, and to be able to learn from failure. I have a great deal of experience in agile fast-paced team environments, and as a result, I am highly adaptable in both independent and collaborative settings. I consider my hunger for both continuous development and user feedback to be some of my strong points, and my skills include prototyping, user research, and wire framing.

In my free time, I enjoy filming and editing vlogs and travel videos for my personal YouTube channel, and I also enjoy spending time at public gardens and museums. I make it a point to visit one whenever I travel to a new city! My favorite experience so far was visiting the Chihuly Garden and Glass museum two years ago in Seattle, and I had the pleasure of seeing it again at the Biltmore in Asheville, North Caroline last summer. One of my favorite things to do is try out good places to eat good food, and recently, I've started trying my hand at baking.

You've heard about me, now let's talk about you. Send me a <a href="mailto:saeunx@gatech.edu">hello</a>. 👋
